What is UNAI?


Universitas Advent Indonesia (UNAI) is a Higher Education institution managed by the Sevent-Day Adventist Chruch of the Western Union of Indonesia. The history of UNAI is that if was founded in Cimindi in 1929 which was originally called Opleiding School der Advent Zending. In the past, this school was founded for the education of the evangelists of the Sevent-Day Adventist Church.


Universitas Advent Indonesia has 6 faculties:

1) Faculties of Philosiphy
2) Faculties of Teacher Training & Education
3) Faculties of Economics
4) Faculties of Nursing
5) Faculties of Mathematics and Natural Sciences
6) Facultiesof Information Technology.
